## About

Hazz Farm is a modern agriculture company focused on combining farming with technology. We are building an ecosystem where agriculture is supported by data, automation, artificial intelligence, and Internet of Things (IoT) to create a more efficient and sustainable future.

Our mission is not only to produce high-quality agricultural products but also to develop digital solutions that empower modern farming.

---

## Vision

> To become a leading Indonesian agritech company by integrating modern agriculture, IoT, artificial intelligence, and data-driven decision making.
